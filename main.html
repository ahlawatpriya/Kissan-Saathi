Cropping Advice & Seasonal Calendar

Essential Crop Recommendations

Rice Cultivation

  • Optimal planting time: June-July (Kharif season)
  • Soil requirement: Clayey or loamy with good water retention
  • Recommended varieties: IR64, Swarna, Basmati
  • Water management: Maintain 2-5 cm water depth

Wheat Farming

  • Best planting window: November-December (Rabi season)
  • Soil type: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Popular varieties: HD2967, PBW343, Lok1
  • Irrigation: 4-5 critical stages
Always consider local weather conditions and consult agricultural experts for region-specific advice.

Farming Tips
Crop Rotation

Rotate your crops annually to maintain soil fertility and reduce pest infestations. A good rotation might include legumes, leafy vegetables, and root crops.

Water Management

Use drip irrigation or soaker hoses to deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing evaporation and water waste. Water early in the morning for best results.

Pest Control

Introduce beneficial insects like ladybugs and lacewings to control pests naturally. Use companion planting to deter pests without chemicals.

Soil Health

Regularly add organic matter like compost to improve soil structure. Test your soil annually to monitor pH and nutrient levels.
